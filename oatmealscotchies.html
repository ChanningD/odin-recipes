Oatmeal Scotcihes

Dsecription

In this scotchies cookies recipe, don't even think that you can eat only one! Boy, are these cookies fabulous! They are so moist and chewy. If you prefer a crisper cookie, just bake for a couple of minutes longer. These are great cookies to send overseas to military troops.

Ingredients

    1 1/4 cups unsalted nutter at room temperture
    3/4 cups brown sugar
    1/2 cup white sugar
    1 egg
    1 teaspoon vannilla extract
    1 1/2 cups all-purspose-flour
    1 teaspon baking soda
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    3 cups quick-cooking rolled
    1 cup butterscotch chips
    1/2 cup sweetened flaked cocount(Optional)

Direcctions

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    In a mixing bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, white sugar, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.
    Next, beat in flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and rolled oats until thoroughly mixed.
    Then, stir in butterscotch chips and coconut.
    Lastly, drop dough by tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto the baking sheets.
    Bake cookies in the preheated oven until bottoms are light brown, 7 to 8 minutes.If you prefer crunchier cookies, bake 9 to 10 minutes.
    Once reday take out of the oven and let them cool for 1 to 2 mintues.
